Frequently Asked Questions

Basic Questions
What is PHINMAEDUHUB?

Phinmaeduhub is an online study platform designed for students to access video lessons provided by their teachers. The platform supports both RAD and FLEX students, offering study resources aligned with their curriculum.

How do I get started with Phinmaeduhub?

To get started, simply sign up using your student or teacher credentials, log in to your account, and explore the video lessons and study materials available on the platform.

Who can use Phinmaeduhub?

Phinmaeduhub is primarily intended for students and teachers of Phinma University of Iloilo, but it can also be beneficial for anyone looking to learn from the available video lessons.

Account

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in the required information, such as your student ID, name, email address, and password. Once you submit your details, you'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

To log in, go to the Phinmaeduhub homepage and click on the "Log In" button. Enter your student ID, email address, and password, then click "Submit" to access your account.

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Teacher-Specific Questions

Students
